Data Approval & Review Automation System

Replaced a chaotic manual approval chain (emails, follow-ups, no accountability) with a fully automated multi-layer review workflow that ensures only verified, approved data reaches the repository.

0
Manual follow-ups. Fully automated end-to-end
+

The Problem

Before this system existed, approving data was entirely manual. Someone would collect data, then begin a chain of emails to stakeholders and upper management, each person waiting to hear from the previous, no visibility into status, continuous follow-up required, and no record of who approved what or who was responsible for any given change. The process was slow, error-prone, and completely unauditable.

What I Did

Ran discovery with operations and compliance stakeholders to map the full approval journey. Designed a configurable workflow engine where approval order and approvers can be set per data type. Built out edge case flows too: rejected data is flagged and returned with comments, and when an approver is unavailable the system automatically escalates or delegates to an alternate. Wrote all PRDs, collaborated on UI design, and drove the build through QA and launch.

Impact

The entire approval process now runs automatically. The system notifies each approver in sequence, passes to the next on approval, and only pushes data to the repository once the full chain is complete. Every action is timestamped and logged: who approved, who rejected, who delegated, and why. Manual coordination and follow-up dropped to zero. Data in the repository is now fully traceable and audit-ready.
